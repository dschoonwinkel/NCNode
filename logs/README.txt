Logs are saved in this directory.

This includes

config.log - General log output of the NCNode program. Contains
the logs of all nodes in the mininet topology. Good for general
debugging, or capturing network addresses etc.

sender*.log - ITGSend specific logs. Binary file, not human readable,
use ITGDec to decode contents.
receiver*.log - ITGSend specific logs. Logical mirror of sender.log
